Self Services

If you're a "do-it-yourself" type of person, our car wash has all the self services you need to make your ride shine and to get the job done your way. Our car wash provides three self service bays, four vacuums, and a variety of auto cleaning products. Self service bays are available 24/7.  Each bay is equipped with high pressure spray wands and overflowing foaming brushes that contain a clean pleasant scent. All bays and equipment are wiped, cleaned and maintained daily. In addition, on-site attendants are available from 9am to 5pm to answer your questions and provide you with assistance, if needed.

Self Service selections include: Wash, Foaming Brush, Rinse, Wax and Spot Free Rinse.
